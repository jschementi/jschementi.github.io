To make this not a novel ... Friday night my computer started to act weird. I backed up all my data to another location of the drive where it would be safe, but I didn't think to get it off my computer and onto something else like another hard drive or DVDs. That was my mistake. Something went wrong and I lost access to all my files. There are still on my hard drive but I can't get to them. I'm sending the hard drive to a data recovery company to see if they can fix it, but it's going to cost me an arm and a leg if they can recover the data. I hope the last 7 years of my work and history are not gone .... ... moral of the story ... back up your data. Even if it's every 6 months, do it. Burn your stuff to DVD. It would have only taken 8 DVDs to back up my data before I messed it up. 8 DVDs + time costs a lot less than $1000. If you want to make your life easier, buy an external backup HD. Just do it, cause if you don't it will happen to you. I use to but stopped, and now look what happened.
