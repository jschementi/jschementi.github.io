Lang.NET was a ton of fun! The room was constantly packed and the talks rocked. Though, I think the most interesting stuff happened in-between the talks, as usual. =)

Reactions

From a informative overview from Mary Jo Foley, a handful of articles, and tons of blog posts from everyone who attended, Lang.NET was definitely buzzing the blogosphere.

We passed around little surveys at the end of the event, and the feedback was great!

John Lam has a great wrap-up of Lang.NET on his blog, complete with his candid capturing of the event.
